# Welcome to FamSphere 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

**Your family organization hub**

FamSphere helps families stay organized, track goals, and celebrate achievements together.

---

## 📱 What is FamSphere?

FamSphere is a family engagement app that makes it easy for parents and children to:
- **Track goals and habits** with points and streaks
- **Stay organized** with a smart calendar system  
- **Communicate** through searchable family chat
- **Celebrate progress** together

The app adapts intelligently for single-child and multi-child families, providing personalized experiences that motivate everyone.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### For Parents

**Monitor Progress**

### For Kids

## ✨ Main Features

### 📊 Dashboard
Your family command center with:
- Family overview and quick stats
- Recent activity and completions
- Upcoming deadlines
- Quick actions for common tasks

### 🎯 Goals
Track habits, earn points, and build streaks with:
- Daily or weekly habits
- Deadlines for one-time goals
- Milestones to break down larger goals
- Points earned for each completion
- Detailed statistics and patterns

### 📅 Responsibility Timeline
Know who owns what and when it's due:
- **🚗 Pickups**: Time-critical child logistics
- **📅 Events**: General family scheduling
- **🎯 Deadlines**: Goal accountability with consequences
- Color-coded urgency indicators
- Recurring events with custom patterns

### 💬 Family Chat
More than messaging — a searchable family record:
- Search past conversations instantly
- Pin important messages (rules, reminders)
- Filter between family and system messages
- System notifies you of approvals and milestones

---

## 🎮 How Points & Streaks Work

### Points ⭐
Earn points by completing goals. Each goal has a point value (1-100) set when created. Points accumulate over time and determine leaderboard rankings.

### Streaks 🔥
Complete a goal on consecutive days to build a streak. Streaks reset if you skip a day. Reach milestones at 3, 7, 14, 30, 50, and 100 days!

---

## 🎯 Goal Approval Process

1. Child creates a goal with details
2. Parent receives a notification badge
3. Parent reviews the goal in the approval queue
4. Parent approves or rejects with feedback
5. Child can start tracking once approved

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

- **Pin important messages**: Long-press chat messages to pin family rules or decisions
- **Search chat**: Find past conversations about specific topics
- **View statistics**: Tap 'View Statistics' on any goal to see patterns
- **Create recurring events**: Set up weekly activities like sports practice
- **Check approval badge**: Parents, watch for the orange badge on the Goals tab

## 📋 Calendar Tabs Explained

### Pickups 🚗
Time-critical logistics like school pickups and dropoffs. Shows who's handling it.

### Events 📅
General family scheduling like appointments, practices, and gatherings. Shows who added it.

### Deadlines 🎯
Goal deadlines with urgency indicators. Shows who owns it and what's at stake (points, streaks).

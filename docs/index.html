Overview

FamSphere is built to help families coordinate daily life with clarity and trust. The app focuses on structured family workflows such as goals, responsibilities, calendars, reminders, and family chat. FamSphere is not a public social network and is not designed for discovery or communication outside your family group.

Data we collect

FamSphere stores only the information needed to provide the app experience for your family group. This typically includes:

  • Family profiles: family member display name, role (parent or child), and optional color/avatar choices.
  • Goals and responsibilities: goal titles, milestones, point values, status, approvals, streaks, and completion history.
  • Calendar items: event titles, dates/times, notes, ownership (parent/child), recurrence, and optional reminders.
  • Family chat content: messages, timestamps, and basic message flags such as pinned or important.
  • App preferences: settings that control app behavior and presentation on your device.
Important: FamSphere does not require you to create a separate FamSphere account. Your family sharing and access are based on your Apple ID and iCloud configuration.

Apple ID and iCloud account information

FamSphere uses your Apple ID and iCloud account status to enable cloud sync and family sharing. FamSphere does not receive your Apple ID password. Apple manages Apple ID authentication and account security.

Data we do not collect

FamSphere is built to avoid unnecessary data collection. Specifically:

  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No sale of personal information.
  • No public profiles or public discovery features.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites.
  • No location tracking as part of core features.

How data is used

Your data is used only to provide the app features you expect:

Goals and responsibilities
Track creation, approvals, completions, points, milestones, and streak progress.
Calendar and reminders
Create events, show upcoming schedule, and trigger reminders you choose.
Family communication
Share updates within your family group using private in-app chat.
Role-based guardrails
Apply parent and child permissions to keep coordination safe and intentional.

Sharing and access control

FamSphere is designed around private family groups. A parent can create a family group and share access via an invitation link. Family members join using their Apple ID, similar to how many Apple sharing experiences work.

FamSphere supports role-aware permissions:

  • Parents: can edit goals, approve or reject requests, and manage family membership and access.
  • Children: can create and complete goals and participate in family chat. Certain actions such as deleting key items may require parental approval based on app configuration.

Access depends on iCloud and CloudKit availability, Apple ID status, and your device configuration.

Notifications

FamSphere can send optional notifications for:

  • Chat messages within your family group
  • Goal updates such as approvals or completions
  • Event reminders such as 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours, or 1 day before an event

Notifications are controlled by your device settings. You can enable or disable FamSphere notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Security

FamSphere is designed to keep family data private and protected. Data is stored using Apple CloudKit and iCloud services. Apple provides encryption and security controls for data in transit and at rest as part of iCloud and CloudKit.

FamSphere does not attempt to circumvent Apple security protections. FamSphere does not sell data or provide data to advertising networks.

Security depends on your Apple ID security practices. We recommend enabling two-factor authentication on your Apple ID and keeping iOS updated.

Children‚Äôs privacy

FamSphere is designed for family use, including by children, within a private family group. There are no public profiles, and FamSphere is not intended for communication with strangers. Parents manage family membership and can review or modify goals and calendar items depending on role permissions.

Data retention and deletion

FamSphere stores data as long as your family group continues to use the app, unless you delete it. Deleting items inside the app removes them from your family data store. Removing a family member or deleting the family group may remove shared access, depending on how the family share is configured.

You may also remove FamSphere data by managing iCloud storage for the app in iOS Settings.

Third-party services

FamSphere relies on Apple services to provide cloud sync, sharing, and notifications, including:

  • Apple iCloud and CloudKit (data storage and sharing)
  • Apple Push Notification service (notifications)

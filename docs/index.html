Overview

FamSphere is designed to help families coordinate daily life with clarity, structure, and trust. The app focuses on private, family-only workflows such as goals, responsibilities, calendars, reminders, and family chat.

FamSphere is not a public social network and is not designed for discovery, advertising, or communication outside your family group.

Apple infrastructure
Data is stored using Apple iCloud and CloudKit services associated with your Apple ID.

Data we collect

  • Family profiles: display name, role (parent or child), and optional avatar or color.
  • Goals and responsibilities: titles, milestones, points, status, approvals, and history.
  • Calendar items: event titles, dates, times, notes, recurrence, and reminders.
  • Family chat: messages, timestamps, and basic message flags.
  • App preferences: settings that control app behavior on your device.
FamSphere does not require a separate account. Access and sharing are based on your Apple ID and iCloud configuration.

Data we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers
  • No sale of personal information
  • No public profiles or discovery
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • No background location tracking

Notifications

FamSphere may send optional notifications for chat messages, goal updates, and calendar reminders such as 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, or 1 day.

Notifications can be managed or disabled at any time in iOS Settings.
